## 770 BCE - 475 BCE: Spring and Autumn Period

Taoism: Lao Tzu

Buddhism (India): Siddhartha Gautama

Art of War: Sun Tzu

## 221 BCE - 206 BCE: Qin Dynasty

First emperor: Qin Shi Huang

Origin of China's name

## 206 BCE - 220: Han Dynasty

## 220 - 265: Three Kingdoms

Shu (SW): Liu Bei

Wei (N): Cao Cao

Wu (SE): Sun (clan)

Mongols

## 1368 - 1644: Ming Dynasty

Writing of _Journey to the West_

Mao Zedong, Deng Xiaoping, Xi Jinping
